30th DCSS win: Octopode Cinder Acolyte of Jiyva

2023-03-28 #dcss #games

I successfully ascended with my 30th win in the Dungeon Crawl: Stone Soup roguelike, playing an Octopode Cinder Acolyte of Jiyva. This was a really fun run, relying on Statue Form and Dragon's Call by the end. Dragon's Call, in particular, seems like one of the most powerful, if not the most powerful, spells.
