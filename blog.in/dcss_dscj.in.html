I had an amazing Dungeon Crawl: Stone Soup win with a great heap of luck. I played a Demonspawn Conjurer with a heap of lucky mutations and incidents, including:

  • Three early acquirement scrolls and a potion of experience
  • Worshipping Vehumet from a faded altar - the perfect conjurer god
  • Mutating magic regeneration early
  • Mutating paralyzing spells
  • Mutating spirit shield, which 2nd level comes with HP regen converted to MP regen when low on MP

By the end of the game, it felt like I needed almost no downtime: with Vehumet and all my sources of MP regen I could spam 4th level spells indefinitely: Iskenderun's Mystic Blast is very powerful when you can cast it every turn for free, and Plasma Beam was my next spell of choice. I wonder if demonspawn mutations are affected by skills or stats, because almost everything I mutated just made the caster playstyle stronger.

A few more notes:

  • Maxwell's Capacitive Coupling is a great spell. I'd cast it once, often paralyzing the enemy due to my mutations, then regenerate almost all the MP it took to cast by the time it fired off and obliterated the nearest foe. I deleted four orbs of fire in a row using just this spell.
  • Brom's Barrelling Boulder combined with Iskenderun's Mystic Blast let me destroy enemies with great MP efficiency without them ever touching me once.
  • Plasma Beam once again takes the title of most used spell: it delivers unparalleled damage and is a beam spell!
